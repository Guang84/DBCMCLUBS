  • ABOUT:

    • The All India Catholic University Federation (AICUF) is a movement of University students who stand for service and liberation of all human persons, as Jesus did, irrespective of caste, gender language or belief. Its aim is to enable students to develop a critical awareness and analysis of the Indian and world situation to cultivate a deep and genuine concern for the oppressed and marginalized and to effectively commit themselves to the task of building a new and just society.
