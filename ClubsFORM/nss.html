  • ABOUT:

    • The main aim of the NSS(National service Scheme) is to educate community the need for social commitment through community service. They get ample opportunities to come in touch with the Neighbourthood communities to channelise their enery through constructive social action and to become aware of social problems and needs. The college organize a variety of programmes for the volunteers to develop their personality. The college has three NSS units - Unit I for Degree students, Unit II and III. The membership is voluntary. Each member has to attend 240 hours of regular activities beside a compulsory special camp for 10 days to avail certificates and other benefits form NSS.
