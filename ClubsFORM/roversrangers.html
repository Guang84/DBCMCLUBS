  • ABOUT:

    • Scouts & Guides movement is an international educational movement for young people open to all without distinction of caste, creed, race or origin started in 1907 in Brown Sea, London. Scouts & Guides is a voluntary, non-political organization. The crew members of this international organization above 18 years of age are known as Rovers and Rangers. Rovers and Rangers channel the energy of the youth in the right direction by making them disciplined, punctual, service-minded and adventurous. The Rovers and Rangers inculcated the spirit of service among students.

    To motivate the young students about the importance of discipline in moulding a balanced personality .
  • To maintain discipline in the college campus.
  • To be always ready to offer service for the society.
  • To arouse a sense of international brotherhood among the students.
